Ventilation

Ventilate your home by getting fresh air into your home, filtering the air that is there, and improving air flow.

Improving ventilation can help you reduce virus particles in your home and keep COVID-19 from spreading.

Good ventilation, along with other preventive actions, can help you and others from getting and spreading COVID-19 and other respiratory viruses.
